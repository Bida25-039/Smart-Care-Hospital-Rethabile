What to Do in a Medical Emergency

  1. Stay calm and ensure the safety of yourself and others.
  2. Call 997 immediately for an ambulance.
  3. Do not move an injured person unless they are in immediate danger.
  4. If trained, provide basic first aid while waiting for help.
  5. Meet the ambulance at the road if possible.
  6. Bring any medication the patient is currently taking.
  7. Bring the patient's OMANG or Passport if possible.
