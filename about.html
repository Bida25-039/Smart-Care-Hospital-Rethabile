About SmartCare Hospital System

A trusted healthcare facility serving Jwaneng and the Southern District since 1982.

Who We Are

SmartCare Hospital System is a government-registered healthcare facility established in 1982 to serve the mining community and residents of Jwaneng — one of the world's richest diamond-producing towns. Over the decades, we have grown into a full-service referral hospital delivering evidence-based, compassionate care across the Southern District of Botswana.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to every person in Jwaneng and the surrounding communities, regardless of their background or economic status.

Our Vision

To be the leading hospital in Southern Botswana, recognised for excellence in patient care, innovation in health service delivery, and community wellness.
