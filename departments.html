Our Departments

SmartCare offers a full range of medical services for the people of Jwaneng and Southern Botswana.

🩺 1. General Medicine (OPD)

Diagnosis and management of acute and chronic conditions. Walk-ins seen first-come, first-served.

  • Internal medicine consultations
  • Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension)
  • Specialist referrals
📍 Block A, Ground Floor  |  📞 +267 388 0350

🤰 2. Maternity & OB/GYN

Comprehensive women's health: antenatal, labour, delivery, postnatal and gynaecological services.

  • ANC clinic – every Monday & Wednesday
  • Labour ward – 24 hours
  • Family planning services
  • Postnatal follow-up
📍 Block B, Ground Floor  |  📞 +267 388 0370

👶 3. Paediatrics

Healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents from birth to 18 years.

  • Growth monitoring & immunisation
  • Management of childhood illnesses
  • Nutritional support (TFP)
  • Child welfare clinics
📍 Block C, First Floor  |  📞 +267 388 0380

🚑 4. Casualty & Trauma

24/7 emergency and trauma services. Triage on arrival prioritises critical patients.

  • Emergency resuscitation
  • Wound care and suturing
  • Fracture management
  • Poisoning and overdose management
📍 Block A, Emergency Entrance  |  📞 +267 388 0400  |  Open 24/7

🦷 5. Dental Services

General dental care for children and adults including extractions, fillings, and oral health education.

  • Dental examinations
  • Extractions and fillings
  • Oral hygiene education
📍 Block D, Ground Floor  |  📞 +267 388 0390

🔬 6. Radiology & Imaging

Diagnostic imaging including X-ray and ultrasound. Doctor referral required.

  • X-ray (plain & chest)
  • Obstetric ultrasound
  • Abdominal ultrasound
📍 Block A, First Floor  |  📞 +267 388 0410

💊 7. Pharmacy

Dispensing of prescribed medications. Government Essential Drug List medicines available.

  • Prescription dispensing
  • ARV dispensing
  • Chronic medication refills
  • Patient medication counselling
📍 Block A, Ground Floor  |  ⏰ Mon–Fri 08:00–18:00

🩸 8. HIV/AIDS & Wellness (IDCC)

Confidential HIV testing, counselling, and ARV treatment through the IDCC.

  • HIV Counselling & Testing (HCT)
  • ARV initiation and monitoring
  • TB/HIV co-management
  • PMTCT services
📍 Block E (private entrance)  |  📞 +267 388 0420

🏋️ 9. Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation and physical therapy for injury, surgery recovery, or chronic musculoskeletal conditions.

  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Stroke rehabilitation
  • Sports injury management
  • Paediatric physiotherapy
📍 Block D, First Floor  |  📞 +267 388 0430
